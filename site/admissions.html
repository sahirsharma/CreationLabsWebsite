Project Proposals

RAL

Robotics & Autonomy Lab

The Robotics & Autonomy Lab focuses mainly on engineering robots that can operate and interact with humans in varying environments. The members are pushing the envelope of what is possible in the domain of autonomy. In addition, we design and build a whole range of unmanned air vehicles, quadcopters and ground bots.

Submit your proposal
ET  

Emerging Technologies Lab

The Emerging Technologies Laboratory (the ETL), based in the Creation Labs is a collective effort to create, extend, and adapt best-of-class computer & electronic technologies. We foster innovation by encouraging experimentation and collaboration. The ideas that emerge are developed with the intent of making a significant contribution to the community.

Submit your proposal
ME 

Mechanical Lab

The Mechanical Lab works on design, analysis, manufacturing and of course racing of state-of-the-art road and off-road vehicles with the aid of advanced software and tools. We have an interdisciplinary group composed of expertise in mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, computer science and mathematics among others.

Submit your proposal
DS  

Digital Sciences Lab

The Digital Sciences laboratory is interested in all aspects of computer science, design & multimedia. The projects currently curated into the lab range from mobile applications & data science systems to augmented reality based operating systems.

Submit your proposal
MD  

Medical Devices Lab

The Medical Devices Lab set up in The Creation Labs is working to improve medical devices by combining the expertise and strengths of the academic institution and industrial partners through collaborative efforts. Translation of laboratory research into formal product development and clinical trials is a primary focus of the laboratory in order for the technological advancements to be utilized clinically to benefit those in need.

Submit your proposal
